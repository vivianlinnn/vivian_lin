COGS 127: realTalk

Case Study Overview

Teenagers who use social media struggle to represent themselves authentically due to societal pressure to curate a perfect image, leading to self-conscious posting behaviors and reduced confidence in self-expression.

User Research

User 1: Lien, 17

Daily time on Social Media: 3 Hours

Lien is an avid social media user including BeReal. She often struggles with meeting societal beauty standards which led her to take a long-term break from social media when it starts affecting her. Lien appreciates the app’s mission towards a more carefree and authentic experience, but also raised the issue of the two minute timer not giving users enough time to snap their BeReals especially when the app crashes or the user is busy at the moment. Because of preexisting societal judgment from the online community towards teenagers, the timer only spiked their anxiety of posting for fear of being labeled “late” and limits user control/freedom, which is a main heuristic for a successful design according to Nielsen. Lien thinks the current constraints that attempt to keep the authenticity just feel forced and scare her. Therefore, she would like to see a feature that would encourage people to use BeReal the way it was designed, maybe making it more fun or less stressful due to the timer.

User 2: Jyanna, 17

Daily time on social media: 4 hours 40 minutes

Jyanna is another active social media user, but has not used BeReal before. The user research phase of this project marks her first time using the app. Jyanna never struggled with self esteem issues or mental health and is confident with her online presence. She does not feel vulnerable to issues of comparison or societal beauty standards and therefore, has never felt the need to take a break from social media or felt overwhelmed with any content they see of others. She appreciates the minimalistic design although the pictures did not turn out the way she expected, and being able to express her authentic self as the post is only shared with friends. However, she finds the user experience very boring especially because when very few of her friends use the platform and recommended allowing the user to find suggested posts from other users outside of their followings.
